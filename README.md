# My Research

Welcome to my research repository. This space serves as a central hub for my ongoing academic projects, preliminary drafts, and finalized research work. My goal with this repository is to keep my developments organized as I progress with my research activities.

 I have structured this repository into two primary sections:

### 📂 Ongoing Works
This folder contains projects that are currently in an elementary or active status. Here you will find:
*   Initial ideas and exploratory sketches.
*   Drafts, notes, and experimental code.
*   Works-in-progress that are evolving and have not yet reached a finalized version.

### 📂 Completed Projects
This folder is dedicated to research that has reached a mature stage. It includes:
*   Preprints and finalized papers.
*   Work that has been completed and finalized in general.
*   Clean, documented code used for simulations or verification.
